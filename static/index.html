Overview

This portfolio is to showcase all the work and progress I made over the week at my Capgemini work experience placement, which I have made from scratch using static HTML and CSS. Throughout the week, I engaged in a variety of group projects and workshops, allowing myself to gain a wide range of skills and experience within IT and Business, such as project management, and gaining a better understanding of AI. You can click on each entry below to view more information about what I did, as well as view evidence of my work.


About Me

I'm a passionate and inquisitive A-Level student studying Music, Maths, Computing, and EPQ at the Sixth Form College Farnborough. I am very driven by technology and find that I have a knack for quickly mastering new technologies and systems. About Capgemini

I would describe Capgemini as a leading global IT services provider that focuses on digital transformation and solving problems with innovative technologies. They are known for their expertise in AI, cloud computing, data analytics, and digital engineering as well as their experience in the financial sector. During my time at Capgemini, I had the pleasure of seeing how a global IT company can work efficiently to deliver effective solutions to their clients. Such clients include well-known organisations such as Heathrow Airport, Barclays, HMRC, and Scottish Water.
